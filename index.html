I am a senior researcher at the Information Futures Lab at Brown University. Most recently, I have worked on improving our understanding of how people discuss the health impact of climate change on themselves in different countries using social media data and machine learning. The findings of this ongoing research, which the Rockefeller Foundation generously supports, will help climate change advocates navigate the narrative landscape and improve their intervention designs.
