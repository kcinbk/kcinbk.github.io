Misinformation researcher

I am a senior researcher at the Information Futures Lab at Brown University. Most recently, I have worked on further our understanding of how people discuss the impact of climate change on their health in different countries using user-generated content from social media and machine learning. The findings of this ongoing research, which the Rockefeller Foundation generously supports, will help climate change advocates navigate the narrative landscape and improve their intervention designs.

Some of my previous works on misinformation and disinformation can be found on the archive of First Draft, where I published my tracking and reporting throughout the Covid-19 pandemic and the 2020 election.
